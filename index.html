I started as a data entry operator, processing forms and updating CRM systems. After proving myself on the production floor, I was promoted to Team Leader — a role I've held for nearly a decade, supervising operations and managing workflow efficiency.

Leading teams taught me something crucial: you can't improve what you can't measure. I saw patterns in error rates, bottlenecks in processing times, and opportunities hidden in daily production data. That's when I shifted my focus to analytics — not as an escape from operations, but as a way to solve operational problems more effectively.

Now I combine hands-on operational experience with technical skills in Excel, SQL, Tableau, PowerBi,and currently expanding capabilities in Python. I build dashboards that track real metrics, analyze trends that actually matter, and create solutions grounded in how work really happens.
